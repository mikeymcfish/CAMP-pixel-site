REGISTRATION/COST
I'm having trouble viewing the mobile version of your registration site. What's wrong?
Our site is not optimized for mobile devices. Please use a desktop, laptop, or tablet when checking out.
Can I make my own Combo by combining any 2 Projects?
We've paired up the Projects we offer into an optimal experience of 2 complementary activities: One physical, the other digital. We feel that these 2 projects combined will make the best experience for your campers. If you have ideas for us for the future, please reach out!
How do I know if a Combo is sold out?
You can check our tickets page to view a live update of available slots for each Combo. Reserve as soon as you can to guarantee your spot!
I'm sold out of a Combo! Is there a waiting list?
We aren't currently running waiting lists for our Camp spots. If no other Combos are looking good, please feel free to keep checking our ticketing site. In the event of a cancellation or reschedule, a spot might open back up!
Can I just sign up for a Physical Project? Or a Digital Project?
Nope. A CAMP/pixel week is only complete with a Combo of a Digital and a Physical Project.
Why are some Combos more expensive than others?
Every new piece of technology we bring in to keep us on the cutting edge comes at a cost. We've meticulously priced our offerings according to the cost of running the 2 particular topics. We make every effort to use resources that our campers can continue to use at home on their own.
How can I save money at CAMP/pixel?
There are many ways to save on a CAMP/pixel summer experience! We have discounts for super-early-bird and early-bird tickets as well as multi-week savings. To be the first to know about any promotions we run on CAMP/pixel, please feel free to subscribe to our mailing list at www.pixelacademy.org/subscribe/.
Will you have at-the-door registration?
We strongly recommend you register in advance for CAMP/pixel! We sold out the vast majority of our spots at last year's CAMP/pixel. When you're ready, don't hesitate to register!

THE CAMP DAY
What do you offer for lunch and snack?
Our lunches are catered by a healthy local vendors. Our snacks are healthy, nutritious, and satisfying.
Can I see a sample schedule of a CAMP/pixel day?
Please visit our schedule to discover what a typical day looks like.
What off-screen activities do campers undertake?
Plenty! Please visit our Day at CAMP/Pixel page to learn more.
What are the CAMP/pixel hours?
We're open from 8:30-5:30 p.m. at all 3 of our locations every weekday during CAMP/pixel. Drop-off is at 8:30-9:15 a.m. and pick-up is from 2:30-3:00 p.m.
Can we pick up late (P.M. extended care)?
Pick-up is at 2:30-3:00 p.m. You're welcome to add on P.M. Extended Care to your purchase, which gives you until 5:30 p.m. to pick up your camper.
Can we drop off early?
No, but we invite you to drop off as early as 8:30 a.m. for no additional charge! We'll ask you to wait nearby if you drop off earlier than 8:30.
How do I know my camper's not just playing video games all day?
At CAMP/pixel, "free time" is earned and not abused. Campers may be found testing a game or mod they design or taking a few minutes for a well-earned fun break, but they all know that it's not what CAMP/pixel is all about.
Can I stay with my child at CAMP/pixel?
We do ask our parents to leave once the check-in process is complete. The first few hours of CAMP/pixel are largely about assimilation. We make every effort to make every camper feel comfortable and excited. We have every camper parent's contact information on hand and never hesitate to contact in the case of anything else.

WHAT TO BRING
Should I pack a lunch for my camper?
We offer a Lunch option as an add-on with our ticket. If you don't select the option, please bring a lunch with your camper every morning!
Should I pack a snack for my camper?
A twice-daily morning and afternoon snack are included in your reservation! You're also welcome to pack snacks of your own if you prefer.
Do I need to bring any technology?
Nope! All of our workshops will be taught using our technology. That said, if your camper would like to bring his or her own technology in the event there's downtime in a day, you're welcome to do so.

MISCELLANEOUS
Can adults participate?
Nope :( But take a look at our job opportunities if you're interested in joining the CAMP/pixel team!
Does Pixel Academy do anything during the year?
Yes! Check out www.pixelacademy.org to view our programs in Brooklyn and Manhattan during the school year.
Are you special needs friendly?
Our Instructors have experience and training with a variety of special needs and learning styles. CAMP/pixel creates an amazing environment for kids and teens who can think and act for themselves. In order to encourage creativity and facilitate a laid-back learning-environment, we try to keep our space an adult-free area (except for our Instructors); we unfortunately cannot currently accommodate anyone that requires an aide in school. If you have any special questions or concerns, please give us a call.
What if I have a special circumstance?
Please let us know as soon as you can so we can make every effort to adjust and prepare accordingly. We're happy to be flexible and do our best to find a solution to any scenario we can!
