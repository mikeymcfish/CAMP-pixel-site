LOCATION
Where is CAMP/pixel?
CAMP/pixel is in lower Manhattan at Pine Street School this summer! Visit our locations page for more information.
Why isn't CAMP/pixel in Brooklyn this summer?
We're taking over an entire 25,000 sq. ft. floor of Pine Street School in downtown Manhattan for a bigger, better, totally unique tech camp experience. We're offering inexpensive bus transportation as a convenient option for our Brooklyn and uptown families; learn more our on locations page!
How can I learn more about your location?
We've worked in-house with Pine Street School (25 Pine) for this entire year, working with their students and the surrounding community in and after school. You can learn more about the school and its beautiful amenities on the school's website.
Can I take a tour of the school before I register?
Yes! Send us an email at info@pixelacademy.org to schedule a good time for you to come by.
Is CAMP/pixel near any public transportation?
Yes! We are within walking distance of the 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, A, C, J, R, and Z trains, the M5 and M15 buses, and the Staten Island Ferry. You can view more information on our locations page.
How can I add bus transportation?
Your CAMP/pixel registration comes with a personal account on our online portal. There, you can view, modify, and add to your camp experience. We email you all the details when you sign up! For more information on buses, visit our locations page.
When can I expect the bus to come by my stop?
We expect our buses to arrive at camp between 8:30 and 9:00 a.m., and depart between 2:30 and 3:00 p.m. Because of that, we project that the stops will take place 7:30-8:00 a.m. and drop-off at 3:30-4:00 p.m. We'll have a more exact idea of when to expect buses at your stop as the summer nears. For more information on buses, visit our locations page.
Is bus available after extended care?
No. Our buses all leave CAMP/pixel for drop-off by 3:00 p.m. Parents adding on Extended Care will need to pick up their camper(s) from Pine Street School by 5:30 p.m.
I don't live near a bus stop. How can I request a new one?
If you'd like to request a different bus stop than we offer, please use our Bus Stop Request form. We can't guarantee that we'll add new stops, but we're constantly monitoring the submissions for areas getting multiple requests!

How much does the Daily Lunch add-on cost?

How much does the Bus Transportation cost?
The Bus Transportation optional add-on is available by week. Cost and more information is available on our locations page. To select an add-on, reserve your CAMP/pixel experience! You'll be contacted soon thereafter with instructions on how to select your add-ons.
Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?
We know that summer camp costs aren't cheap, so we offer several ways to save at CAMP/pixel. These include early-bird, multi-week and sibling discounts, financing options, and a camp crowdfunding resource. Check out our cost page for more deails.
Why are some camps more expensive than others?
Every piece of technology we introduce to CAMP/pixel comes at a cost. We've meticulously priced our offerings according to the cost of running the 2 particular topics. We make every effort to use resources that our campers can continue to use at home on their own. Visit our Cost page for more information on ways to save!
I'm having trouble viewing the mobile version of your registration site. What's wrong?
While our camp site is mobile-friendly, our complex ticketing site is not optimized for viewing on mobile devices. Please use a desktop, laptop, or tablet to view our Tickets page if you're having any issues.
How do I know if a camp is sold out?

AGES
My child is 5. Can he or she join CAMP/pixel?
Unfortunately, we've determined that age 5 is too young for a camper to succeed at CAMP/pixel. Keep us in mind in the future, as we'd love to see kids with such early interest in tech!
My child is 6. Can he or she join CAMP/pixel?
CAMP/pixel is a social group experience. If you feel like your 6-year-old is comfortable working and socializing with a group and curriculum designed for ages 7-9, then we'd welcome them to camp! Please keep in mind that we don't currently offer refunds once your camp week has already started.
My child is 9. For which age group do I sign up?
It's up to you! CAMP/pixel is both a learning and a social experience; you're free to put your camper in whichever age group you think he or she would feel more comfortable.
I want to put my child in a different age group than is advertised. Is that allowed?
We've designed our CAMP/pixel curriculum and activities to reflect ability relative to our 3 age groups: 7-9, 9-11, and 12-14. It's also very much a social experience of working and playing with like-minded individuals. We recommend the age groups we advertise for the optimal camp experience, but you're invited to register your child for whichever age group you think best.
How can teens older than 14 get involved?
Great question! We offer a C.I.T. position for ages 15-17 and summer internships for tech-loving students ages 18+, both of which are great opportunities for learning and leading. Visit the links to learn more and apply!

THE CAMP DAY
What are CAMP/pixel hours?
CAMP/pixel runs from 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. on Monday-Friday during all 10 weeks of the summer. Flexible check-in is at 8:30-9:00 a.m. and pick-up is from 2:30-3:00 p.m. Parents wishing to pick up later can add on P.M. Extended Care, which runs until 5:30 p.m. Learn more on our Day at CAMP/pixel page.
What do you offer for lunch and snack?
Our lunches are catered by a healthy local venders. Our snacks are healthy, nutritious, and satisfying. We'll release more information on provider and menu as the summer nears. Campers are also welcome to pack their own lunches and snacks! Learn more on our Day at CAMP/pixel page.
Can I see a sample schedule of a CAMP/pixel day?
Yes! Please visit our Day at CAMP/pixel page to discover what a typical day looks like and call us if you have any questions.
What off-screen activities do campers undertake?
Plenty! In addition to learning, the camp day is packed with screen breaks, food breaks, physical activities, and more. Please visit our Day at CAMP/pixel page to learn more.
Can we pick up late (P.M. extended care)?
Pick-up is at 2:30-3:00 p.m. You're welcome to add on P.M. Extended Care to your reservation, which gives you until 5:30 p.m. to pick up your camper. Learn more on our Day at CAMP/pixel page.
Can we drop off early?
We invite you to drop off as early as 8:30 a.m. for no additional charge. We'll ask you to wait nearby if you arrive earlier than 8:30 as we won't be staffed to support you at that time. Learn more on our Day at CAMP/pixel page.
Can I stay with my child at CAMP/pixel?
We do ask our parents to leave once the check-in process is complete. The first few hours of each camp are largely about assimilation; we make every effort to inspore a sense of comfort and excitement in each of our campers. We have every parent's contact information on hand and we don't hesitate to call.
How do I know my child is not just playing video games all day?
At CAMP/pixel, "free time" is earned and not abused. Campers may be found testing a game or mod they design or taking a few minutes for a well-earned fun break, but they all know from day 1 that it's not what CAMP/pixel is all about. Learn more on our Day at CAMP/pixel page.

WHAT TO BRING
What does my child need to bring to CAMP/pixel?
If you don't sign up for our daily lunches, we require you to pack a lunch. We have snacks and water here, but you're welcome to back both if you prefer. We use our technology for learning, so no need to bring laptops or iPads. Most importantly, make sure your camper comes with a positive attitude and readiness to learn!
Should I pack a lunch for my camper?
We offer a Lunch option as an add-on with our ticket. If you don't select the option, please bring a lunch with your camper every morning!
Should I pack a snack for my camper?
A twice-daily morning and afternoon snack are included in your reservation! You're also welcome to pack snacks of your own if you prefer.
Do I need to bring any technology?
Nope! All of our workshops will be taught using our technology. That said, if your camper would like to bring his or her own technology in the event there's downtime in a day, you're welcome to do so.
Should my camper bring a flash drive?
Every camper gets a free 8GB flash drive with his or her reservation! Campers will take the drive home with their finished projects loaded on at the end of each camp. No need to bring a flash drive unless your camper has lost the one give to him at the beginning of the week.
What do you do when it rains?
Water and technology: Not a great mix. Make sure your camper is dry at drop-off on rainy days (raincoats, boots)! We tend to avoid going outside on days with inclement weather.

Is CAMP/pixel associated with Pixel Academy?
Yes, we're one and the same! During the year, we are the Pixel Academy, teaching after-school and in-school programs around the 5 boroughs. During the summer, we're CAMP/pixel. Both offer hands-on, future-focused technology learning.
Can adults participate?
Nope :( But take a look at our job opportunities if you're interested in joining the CAMP/pixel team!
Do you do anything during the year?
Yes! Check out www.pixelacademy.org to view our programs in Brooklyn and Manhattan during the school year.
Are you special needs friendly?
Our Instructors have experience and training with a variety of special needs and learning styles. CAMP/pixel creates an amazing environment for kids and teens who can think and act for themselves. In order to encourage creativity and facilitate a laid-back learning-environment, we try to keep our space an adult-free area (except for our Instructors); we unfortunately cannot currently accommodate anyone that requires an aide in school. If you have any special questions or concerns, please give us a call.
What if I have a special circumstance?
