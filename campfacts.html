Technology is becoming more and more necessary for future jobs, rendering the utility of early STEM education at a critically high level. Don't just take our word for it, take the U.S. Department of Education, or NCWIT, or LinkedIn. CAMP/pixel brings cutting-edge, relevant topics of technology recognizable in A.P. and college-level Computer Science classes and breaks them down into intriguing, hands-on experiences for kids ages 7-14. Campers are introduced to a new world of imagination and creativity through inquiry-based learning using professional-grade technology and methods that most students don't get at school.. Whether it's designing on a 3D plane, experimenting with virtual reality, producing creative digital art, or any of our other time-tested curriculum, our project-based approach encourages exploration and creativity while always promoting our Rule #1: Have Fun.

We use technology of the present to help kids create ideas for the future.
