CAMP/pixel programs are for kids and teens ages 7 and up. If you have a younger child who wants to join CAMP/pixel, or have any questions about our summer camp age groups, please shoot us an email at info@pixelacademy.org

About CAMP/pixel
Technology is becoming more and more necessary for future jobs, rendering the utility of early STEM education at a critically high level. Don't just take our word for it, take the U.S. Department of Education, or NCWIT, or LinkedIn. CAMP/pixel brings cutting-edge, relevant topics of technology recognizable in A.P. and college-level Computer Science classes and breaks them down into intriguing, hands-on experiences for kids ages 7-14. Campers are introduced to a new world of imagination and creativity through inquiry-based learning using professional-grade technology and methods that most students don't get at school.. Whether it's designing on a 3D plane, experimenting with virtual reality, producing creative digital art, or any of our other time-tested curriculum, our project-based approach encourages exploration and creativity while always promoting our Rule #1: Have Fun.

We use technology of the present to help kids create ideas for the future.
For more information, visit CAMP/about.

CAMP/pixel offers programs for our youngest learners, ages 7-9. We align with early elementary-level academic and reading levels to create a visual, hands-on learning experience for kids ready to imagine with us.

Each of our topics is available in fun, intriguing, inquiry-based activities. Every camper will spend his or her learning time asking questions, imagining and building projects, and being assisted by our expert instructors. Built into the camp day are plenty of screen breaks, physical activities, food breaks, and opportunity to learn and play with one another.

CAMP/pixel is building an elite team of well-qualified high-school students ages 15 - 17. Join us to master an aspect of technology, gain relevant future application material, learn some crucial early job skills, and have a ton of fun working with kids and like-minded teens while you're at it. We're running this program to assist teens in acquiring leadership skills, giving them valuable training and helping them to set personal goals and work towards achieving them.

Counselor In Training
(C.I.T.) Position
Teens ages 15 - 17
4-week project-based summer program for tech-loving teens
No cost to apply or participate
Hands-on experience with cool, cutting-edge technology
Experience and training that will help you in school, college, and beyond

The CAMP/pixel C.I.T. position is a perfect blend of LEARNING and LEADING for teens with a passion for technology and education. Our C.I.T.'s spend some of their day honed in on technology projects, learning new skills and working with our expert instructors to program, code, produce, design, and invent new products. The rest of the day is spent working with campers in a leadership role to help them learn a new skill, complete a camp challenge, or just have fun.

Learning
  • Cutting-edge and professional grade technology.
  • Mentoring and skills tutoring from our expert instructors.
  • Leadership training and teamwork experience
  • Guidance towards personal goal-setting and self-efficiency.
Leading
  • Hands-on skill development, helping kids learn technology.
  • Opportunities to lead activities and screen-breaks during camp.
  • Job-relevant experience leading and managing tasks.
  • Assisting instructors, learning teaching and leadership skills.
