CAMP/pixel is building an elite team of well-qualified high-school students ages 15 - 17. Join us to master an aspect of technology, gain relevant future application material, learn some crucial early job skills, and have a ton of fun working with kids and like-minded teens while you're at it. We're running this program to assist teens in acquiring leadership skills, giving them valuable training and helping them to set personal goals and work towards achieving them.

Counselor In Training
(C.I.T.) Position
Teens ages 15 - 17
4-week project-based summer program for tech-loving teens
No cost to apply or participate
Hands-on experience with cool, cutting-edge technology
Experience and training that will help you in school, college, and beyond

The CAMP/pixel C.I.T. position is a perfect blend of LEARNING and LEADING for teens with a passion for technology and education. Our C.I.T.'s spend some of their day honed in on technology projects, learning new skills and working with our expert instructors to program, code, produce, design, and invent new products. The rest of the day is spent working with campers in a leadership role to help them learn a new skill, complete a camp challenge, or just have fun.

Learning
  • Cutting-edge and professional grade technology.
  • Mentoring and skills tutoring from our expert instructors.
  • Leadership training and teamwork experience
  • Guidance towards personal goal-setting and self-efficiency.
Leading
  • Hands-on skill development, helping kids learn technology.
  • Opportunities to lead activities and screen-breaks during camp.
  • Job-relevant experience leading and managing tasks.
  • Assisting instructors, learning teaching and leadership skills.
