CAMP/topics
This summer, campers work on two projects throughout their CAMP/pixel experience, one PHYSICAL and one DIGITAL. Together, these projects form a powerful, fundamental look into a realm of technology campers may recognize later in college-level computer science and engineering classes. Each topic includes cutting-edge technology, expert instructors, 21st-century skills, a hands-on, inquiry-based learning experience unique to CAMP/pixel.
CUT/scenes
Digital Project: Stop-Motion Animation
Campers are introduced to the fundamentals of movie making, animating, and narrative structure on industry-standard animation software. Campers create their own unique stop-motion stories they can share with family and friends after camp!

Physical Project: Vinyl Stickers
Campers design and produce vinyl stickers! Campers explore product design, vector tracing, and principles of professional design software. Campers leave with custom stickers cut out by our electronic vinyl cutting machine!
