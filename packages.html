Get the most out of your CAMP/pixel experience, and start your child on a path to self-identity as a future "techspert". Each of our curated CAMP/packages combines several of our project-fueled offerings into 3- to 4-week complete ideas of fundamental knowledge of a realm of technology. Choose from our Coder package, Maker package, Minecraft Master package, Artist Package, or Game Designer Package, all accessible for campers ages 7-11 with no prior experience.
