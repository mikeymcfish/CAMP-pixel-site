CAMP/pixel is at Pine Street School, a new, state-of-the-art private school in the Financial District. The school offers modern, sunlit-classrooms and friendly, open common areas for activities. Pixel Academy is there year-round, running in-school and after-school programs to the students and community. For full information on the school, visit www.pinestreetschool.com.
Sign-up for transportation with Camp/Pixel! Extra convenience for parents and extra time together for campers!
Our exclusive Camp/Pixel buses make stops in Manhattan and Brooklyn and offer the easiest way to make sure campers are picked up and dropped off on time and alongside their fellow campers. Sign up now to secure your spot for the summer! Just select the route that works best for you at checkout!*

Transportation includes both pickup and dropoff for $150 a week (that's just $30 a day!) Campers are accompanied by Camp/Pixel CPR/First-Aid-certified staff the whole way.
